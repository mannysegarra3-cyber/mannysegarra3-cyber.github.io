About Me

I am Manny Segarra 3 — the Agile Knight. A Senior Scrum Master and Agile Coach with over 16 years in the field, helping teams and organizations move from chaos to clarity.

My work is built on nine values tattooed on my body because they are the foundation of who I am: Honor, Discipline, Respect, Compassion, Loyalty, Wisdom, Truth, Justice, and Courage. These are not guidelines. They are how I lead every day.

I hold the Scrum Alliance AI Certification for Scrum Masters and formerly held SAFe SPC Level 5 — deep experience in scaled Agile, organizational transformation, and change management at the enterprise level.

I am also a man of faith. My work and my values are not separate — they are the same thing. I meditate daily. I know myself. That self-knowledge is what makes every coaching conversation real.
